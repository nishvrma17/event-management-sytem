Services Provided

Event Planning and Coordination

Concept Creation: Developing themes and concepts for events.

Timeline Management: Creating schedules and ensuring everything runs on time.

Budget Planning: Helping clients plan and stay within budget.

Vendor Coordination: Collaborating with decorators, caterers, photographers, and other vendors.

On-Site Management: Supervising the event to ensure smooth execution.

Venue Services

Research and Shortlisting: Identifying venues that align with the event's theme, scale, and budget.

Venue Visits: Organizing walkthroughs for clients to evaluate potential options.

Contract Negotiation: Handling terms, rates, and agreements with the venue provider.

Accessibility Considerations: Ensuring the venue is accessible for all attendees, including those with disabilities.

Design And Decoration

Thematic Decoration: Customizing event themes like weddings, corporate galas, or birthday parties.

Floral Arrangements: Designing beautiful floral installations.

Lighting Design: Ambient, stage, and decorative lighting.

Stage Setup: Building stages and backdrops for performances or ceremonies.

Catering and Food Services

Tailored Menus: Creating bespoke menus based on the event's theme, cultural preferences, or dietary restrictions.

Cuisine Specialties: Offering a variety of cuisines (e.g., Italian, Indian, Continental, Asian fusion, etc.).

Seasonal Offerings: Incorporating seasonal ingredients for freshness and flavor.

Tasting Sessions: Arranging pre-event tastings to finalize the menu.
